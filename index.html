Valtellina in Lombardy Italy

The valley next to Lake Como with rich culture and a million activities to do

Activities

Foods

Valtellina is known for its many culinary dishes such as pizzoccheri, schiatt, polenta taragna and many cheeses & chacuterie.

Snowsport

Valtellina is also known for its slopes. Bormio hosts games every year for example. Aprica has the longest nightly lit slope in Europe. Livigno is one of the trendiest places to be, the Couchevel of Italy!

Summer

Valtellina has numerous summer activities. Sport fans are served! There is trekking, kayaking, mushroom picking, hiking, rock climbing, biking and for the less sporty there are wonderful Terme di Bormio and many shopping spots! Lake Como is very close as well.
